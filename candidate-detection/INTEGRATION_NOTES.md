# Integration Notes

Candidate Detection output is designed to feed the authorization-gate harness.

This v0.3 release demonstrates candidate derivation and evidence binding.

Full end-to-end pipeline remains a future step.

Non-claims:

- Candidate Detection != Patch Approval.
- Evidence Binding != Evidence Sufficiency.
- Risk Hint != Risk Score.
- v0.3 does not publish the private core.
