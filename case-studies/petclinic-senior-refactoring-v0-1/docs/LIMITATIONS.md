# Limitations

This package is a pre-execution commitment, not a result report.

It does not show that the refactoring can be executed, that behavior remains preserved, that architecture improves, that the final quality profile passes, that any public branch should be published, or that the system is production-ready.

The counterfactual review was same-agent review. It may demote the plan but is not independent external review.

The digest commitments bind public claims to private Part-1 artifacts. They do not reveal those artifacts and do not prove their quality.
