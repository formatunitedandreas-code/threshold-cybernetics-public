# Public Disclosure Policy

This case study publishes only public-safe pre-execution facts and digest commitments.

Public exact fields include the run ID, selected candidate ID, candidate score, lead, non-authority state, validation counts and explicit non-claims. Sanitized fields describe architecture area, selection rationale, quality dimensions and the planned three-batch shape at a high level.

Private Part-1 artifacts, exact Product path allowlists, exact Product path denylists, local operator observations, raw receipts, full prompts and local paths are not copied into this repository.

Credentials, tokens, cookies, authorization headers, private keys, active grants, active permits and replayable authority are forbidden.

Digest commitments bind this public record to private Part-1 artifacts. They do not reveal those artifacts and do not independently prove correctness, completeness or quality.
