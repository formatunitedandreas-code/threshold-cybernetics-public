# Public Claims

## Allowed claims

- One owned-repository governance PR was merged.
- The merge was bound to an exact PR, head SHA, base SHA, required-check state, review state, and squash merge method.
- Two earlier merge attempts failed without repository mutation.
- A review finding caused an additional remediation cycle.
- The final merge was followed by remote-main reconciliation.
- The one-shot merge authority was consumed after use.
- No finite batch grant was activated.

## Explicit non-claims

- The system is fully autonomous.
- The system is production-ready.
- The system proves general AI safety.
- The system is formally verified.
- The system can safely operate arbitrary repositories.
- The system can merge without human authority.
- The system has upstream authority.
- The system supports autonomous multibatch execution.
- The system is certified or compliant.
- The system eliminates human review.
